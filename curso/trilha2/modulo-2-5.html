Topic 1

😶 Script for unconscious audience

This clientdon't know there's a problem. Trying to sell to him is like offering medicine to those who think they are healthy. Here, you educate before selling.

Approach to unconscious

Objective

Don't sell. Awakening awareness about a problem that he has not yet noticed.

Example script

"Did you know that most restaurants lose up to R$15,000 a month on bad days and don't even realize it? And that's because normally we just look at total revenue and don't calculate what we're missing out on."

Ideal channels

Educational content, social media posts, articles, short videos, free webinars. Don't use a direct approach — he won't respond.

💡 The rule of the unconscious

With an unconscious public,content and sale. You're not selling a product — you're selling awareness of a problem. The product comes later, when he wakes up.

Topic 2
2

😰 Problem-aware script

This clientYou know you have a problem, but you don't know how to solve it. This is the most abundant audience and where consultative selling shines. Here you deepen the pain and position your solution as the logical path.

Script to aware of the problem

VALIDATE PAIN

"You're not the only one. Most [client types] face exactly this. It's normal—but it doesn't have to stay that way."

DEEPER

"Tell me more about how this affects your daily life. How much time/money do you think you waste on this?"

DESIGN CONSEQUENCE

"If it goes on like this for another 6 months, what happens?"

SUBMIT PATH

"There is a structured way to resolve this. Can I show you how other clients have gotten out of this situation?"

📊 Why this is the best audience

The awareness of the problem already hasmotivation to act. He just needs direction. If you make a good diagnosis and show a clear path, the sale is a natural consequence.

Topic 3
3

🔎 Solution-aware script

This clientYou already know what type of solution you need, but you haven't yet decided who to. He is comparing. Your focus here is differentiation and proofs.

Script focused on differentiation

Understand what he already knows

"Have you researched this? What have you seen on the market? What pleased you and what didn't convince you?"

Show the difference

"Most solutions do X. What we do differently is Y — because we understand that [reason connected to the customer's pain]."

Social proof

"[Client name] was in the same situation. He chose us because [differential]. In [deadline], he achieved [result]."

✓ What works

  • Real success stories
  • Clear and specific differences
  • Guarantees and risk reduction
  • honest comparison

✗ What doesn't work

  • Repeat the diagnosis (he already knows)
  • Talking bad about your competitor
  • Being generic about benefits
  • Press without evidence

💡 Practical tip

Always have3 ready-made success storiesto count. Real stories from customers who had the same problem and resolved it with you. Social proof is the most powerful argument at this level.

📦 Script for product conscious

This clientalready know your product. He knows what you do, maybe he has already visited your website or received a referral. What is missing is the final confidence to make the decision.

Elements of final trust

⭐
Testimonials
Results from other customers
🛡️
Guarantee
Zero risk for the customer
🎯
Demonstration
Show working
📊
Numbers
Concrete and calculated ROI

Template script

"You already know what we do. What I can show you are the real results:"

  • ✓"[Client A] went from R$X to R$Y in [deadline]"
  • ✓"We offer a guarantee of [X days] — if it doesn't work, you don't pay"
  • ✓"I can show you live how it works in 10 minutes"

📊 The power of guarantee

A well-structured guarantee canincrease conversion by up to 30%. Not because people return (less than 5% use the guarantee), but because the guarantee eliminates the fear of making the wrong decision.

Topic 5
5

🚀 Script for very conscious

This clientyou already know everything— knows the problem, the solution and your product. He just needs one final push. With him, less and more. Be direct.

Straightforward scripts for the very aware

Direct offer

"You already know everything. The investment is R$X. Do you want to get started?"

With special condition

"If you close by [date], I can include [bonus] at no additional cost. Does that make sense?"

Action question

"What needs to happen to get us started this week?"

✓ Very consciously

  • Be direct and objective
  • Clear and simple offer
  • Bonus or special condition
  • Ask when you want to start

✗ Don't do this

  • Repeat the entire diagnosis
  • Make a long presentation
  • Overexplain
  • Take a while to get to the point

💡 Summary of the 5 levels

Unconscious → Educate. Aware of the problem → Deepen. Aware of the solution → Differentiate. Product awareness → Taste. Very conscious → Offer.The fatal error is using the wrong script for the wrong level.
