Topic 1
1

🚀 High aptitude + high desire

This is theborn salesman. He has a natural ability for communication and persuasion, and wants to be in sales. He likes the game, he likes to close, he likes to hit goals. It's the most accelerated profile - with the right investment, you can become a top performer in weeks, not months.

How to develop this profile

  • ✓ Of growing challenges— If he hits the easy goal, increase it. Tedio kills this profile.
  • ✓ Of autonomy— Don’t micromanage. He needs space to operate.
  • ✓ Of recognition— This profile is fueled by visible achievements.
  • ✓ Prepare for leadership— Could be your next commercial leader.

💡 Common Trap

The most common error with this profile isnot giving enough challenge. He gets bored, stops trying, and you think he's "lost the will." In fact, he needs more, not less.

Topic 2
2

🎭 High aptitude + low desire

This is the mostfrustrating for managers. The person has clear talent - you see that they can convince, that they can read people, that they know how to lead a conversation. But she doesn't want to sell. They may be prejudiced against the area, they may be in the wrong role, or they may have been mistreated in previous experiences.

Possible causes of low will

1

Prejudice with sales

Think "sell and push". He doesn't see sales as a noble profession. He is ashamed to present himself as a salesman.

2

Previous negative experience

Worked in a company with a culture of toxic pressure. It associates sales with suffering and exploitation.

3

Wrong function

The person should be in CS, marketing or product. He has the relational talent but not the commercial profile.

✓ What to do

  • "Frank talk about the lockdown"
  • "Show consultative sales as a path"
  • "Give time to decide (30-60 days)"
  • "Consider relocation if not moving"

✗ What NOT to do

  • "Force sales waiting for talent to appear"
  • "Ignore signs of resistance"
  • "Press with aggressive goals"
  • "Keep the function indefinitely"

📊 Perspective

About30% of sellers from this profilecan be "rescued" when the blockage is worked. The other 70% must be redirected - and this is not failure, it is management intelligence.

Topic 3
3

📚 Low aptitude + high desire

This is therough diamond. He was not born with ease in sales - he is not charismatic by nature, he does not read people instinctively, he does not persuade fluidly. But he really wants it. Arrive early, leave late, ask for help, accept feedback, try again. And the profile that most surprises managers who know how to develop.

The development strategy

📝
Structured scripts
Provide concrete tools to overcome the lack of naturalness
🎯
Intermediate goals
Break big goals into smaller, celebratory achievements
👥
Shadow with top performer
Learning by observing those who are already doing well
🔄
Frequent role play
Practice in a safe environment until you gain confidence

💡 The truth about this profile

Many ofbest sellers on the marketthey started like this. They didn't have natural talent, but they had an unshakable will. The method compensated for their lack of aptitude and, over time, they developed skills that seem natural - but were built.

Topic 4
4

⚠️ Low aptitude + low desire

This is the profile ofcommercial low fit. He doesn't have a natural ability to sell and, more importantly, he doesn't want to develop it. This does not mean that he is a bad professional - it means that the commercial area is not the place where he will perform and be happy.

The cost of keeping it in the wrong place

!

For the person

Daily frustration, anxiety, low professional self-esteem, constant feeling of inadequacy.

!

For the team

Negative energy, below-average results that pull the bar down, the manager's time poorly invested.

!

For the company

Lost revenue, cost of maintenance (fixed salary with no return), wasted opportunity to have someone better in the position.

How to conduct retargeting

  • ✓ Honest and respectful conversation— Bluntly, but with empathy
  • ✓ Show the data— Use the diagnosis as an objective basis
  • ✓ Offer alternatives— If possible, indicate areas where the profile fits
  • ✓ Within a reasonable period of time— Don’t be surprised by an immediate unilateral decision

📊 Reflection

Redirecting someone is notpunish. And recognize that each person has a place where they can shine. Keeping someone in the wrong place out of "pity" or "hope" is true cruelty.

Final Summary

📝Module Summary

What you learned:

  • ✓ The quadrant maps 4 distinct profiles
  • ✓ Each quadrant requires a different strategy
  • ✓ Born salespeople need challenge, not control
  • ✓ Low fit should be redirected, not forced
