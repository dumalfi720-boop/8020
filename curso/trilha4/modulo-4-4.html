🔎 Discover the cause of rejection

Before labeling someone as “sales resistant,” ask:Does this person reject sales or reject the current model?Often, the resistance is to cold calling, not generating revenue. This distinction completely changes the approach.

Resistance diagnosis

  • ✓ Reject the model:"I don't like calling strangers, but I love helping customers who come to me"
  • ✓ Reject the pressure:"I get anxious about monthly goals, but I work well with long-term projects"
  • ✓ Rejects sales:"I don't see myself in any commercial role, I want to do something else"

💡 Practical Exercise

Ask the resister 3 questions: 1) "If you could choose any business role, what would it be?" 2) "What exactly bothers you about the current model?" 3) "At what time of the day do you feel most productive?" The answers reveal the path.

Topic 2
2

🎯 Pre-sales as a path

Pre-sales is the perfect role for those who are good atinvestigate, qualify and prepare, but there is no profile to close. Pre-sales qualifies leads, schedules meetings and prepares opportunities — without the pressure of closing.

What pre-sales does

1

Research and qualification

Investigate the lead, understand the context, validate fit with the product/service. Use objective criteria to qualify.

2

Qualified scheduling

Set up meetings for closers with already qualified leads. Prepares briefing with relevant information.

3

Opportunity preparation

Gathers information, identifies pain, maps decision makers. Deliver a “ready to close” opportunity to the closer.

📊 Perspective

Companies with structured pre-sales have30-40% higher conversion ratein closing, because closers only speak to qualified leads. The resistant that becomes pre-sales multiplies the results of the closers.

Topic 3

🤝 Content and relationship

Some resistant areexcellent communicators in writing and terrible on the phone. For these, the role of creating educational content, nurturing relationships and building brand presence is the ideal place.

✓ Content and relationship functions

  • Create success stories and educational materials
  • Manage newsletter and nurturing automations
  • Maintain relationships with existing customers
  • Produce webinars and technical content

Indirect impact on sales

  • → Leads that consume content convert 5x more
  • → Well-nourished customers renew 80% more frequently
  • → Educational content reduces sales cycle by 30%
  • → Strong brand attracts qualified leads organically

💡 Rule of Thumb

Ask the resister to write a follow-up email to a customer. If the text is clear, empathetic and persuasive, you have found acommercial content creator. Redirect to this function.

📱 Social selling

Social selling and the art of usingsocial networks to build authority, generate trust and attract customersorganically. It's not DM spam — it's strategic presence that makes the customer come to you.

Pillars of social selling

📝
Post consistently
3-5x per week, valuable content about the sector
💬
Engage with intention
Comment on posts from prospects and partners
🤝
Connect with purpose
Add decision makers with personalized message
📊
Measure impact
Track leads generated via social

📊 Why does this matter?

Sellers who use social selling have45% more opportunitiesand are 51% more likely to achieve their goal. The resistant who masters social selling can generate as much pipeline as a traditional closer.

Topic 5
5

💼 Internal consultancy

Internal consultancy is the role for the resistant whohas in-depth technical knowledge but does not want to be on the front line. He supports sellers with analysis, proposal preparation and strategic support.

The internal consultant in practice

1

Preparation of complex proposals

Analyzes the client's scenario, calculates ROI, prepares customized presentations for the closer to use in the meeting.

2

Competition analysis

Maps competitors, differences, weaknesses. Arm the seller with strategic information.

3

Technical support in meetings

Participate in technical meetings with the closer, answer in-depth questions without having to "sell".

💡 The good news

The resistor repositioned as an internal consultant often becomesone of the most valuable team members. He doesn't sell directly, but he makes sales possible that wouldn't happen without his support.
