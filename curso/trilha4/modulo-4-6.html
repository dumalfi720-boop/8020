📊 The seller x awareness level matrix

The most powerful intersection in commercial management: which salesperson to place with which type of customer to maximize conversion and revenue.

🔀 The intersection that defines who does what

The matrix crosses two axes:seller profile(talented, trainable, resilient) andcustomer awareness level(unconscious, problem conscious, solution conscious, product conscious, very conscious). The result is an allocation map that maximizes results.

Matrix overview

Seller / Customer Unconscious Aware of the Problem Solution Aware Product Aware Very Conscious
Talented Ideal Ideal Good Good OK
Trainable Difficult Possible Ideal Ideal Good
Resistant Support Support Support Content Consultancy

💡 How to use this matrix

Print this matrix and place it on your wall. Every time a new lead arrives,classify the level of awareness and allocate it to the most appropriate salesperson profile. In 30 days you will see the difference in the numbers.

Topic 2
2

⭐ Talented: how you perform at each level

Talent shines brightest with clientslow consciousness, where your ability to create emotional connection, generate urgency and conduct complex conversations makes the difference between selling and not selling.

Action by level of consciousness

★

Unconscious → Creative approach

The talented person uses storytelling and provocations to awaken the customer who doesn't even know they have a problem. "Did you know that 80% of companies in your sector are losing money with..."

★

Aware of the problem → Amplify the pain

Ask questions that deepen the perception of the problem. "What happens if you don't resolve this in the next 6 months?"

●

Solution aware → Differentiate

Position the solution as unique. Use cases, differentiators and personal connections to stand out from the competition.

📊 Perspective

With very conscious clients, talent can be "too much" — the client has already researched, already knows what they want, and just needsefficiency and confidence. At this level, the process-trainable can convert as well as the talented.

Topic 3
3

📏 Trainable: what to use at each level

Trainable works best with clients frommedium to high consciousness, where well-defined scripts and processes are sufficient to drive sales. Each level requires a different itinerary.

Scripts by level

  • ✓ Aware of the solution:Comparative demo script — "See how our product specifically solves problem X..."
  • ✓ Product Aware:Value Proposition Script — "Our customers in your industry report Y% improvement in Z..."
  • ✓ Very aware:Closing Script — "Based on what we've discussed, the best option for you is plan X. Can we start next week?"

💡 Rule of Thumb

Create onescript bankorganized by level of consciousness. The coach chooses the right script for each lead before the call. This eliminates improvisation and ensures consistency.

Topic 4
4

🔄 Resistant: where to fit

The resistantHe's not a worse salesperson — he's a professional with different skills. At headquarters, he contributes to support functions that enable sales of other profiles.

Function by client's level of awareness

1

Unaware customers → Educational content

Create articles, posts and materials that awaken the market. Generates demand without direct approach.

2

Customers under evaluation → Research and qualification

Investigate the lead, prepare briefings, qualify opportunities for closers.

3

Very aware customers → Technical consultancy

Participates in technical meetings, answers in-depth questions, prepares customized proposals.

📋 The complete table of actions

This is the most practical tool on this trail. THEfull share tabledefines for each profile x level intersection: the main action, the tool to use, the monitoring metric and the review deadline.

Action template (simplified example)

Talented + Unaware Customer Review: Weekly

Action:Creative cold outreach with storytelling

Tool:Personalized email + LinkedIn

Metric:Response rate > 15%

Trainable + Product Aware Customer Review: Weekly

Action:Structured demo with proposal script

Tool:Demo script + ROI calculator

Metric:Demo conversion rate → proposal > 40%

Tough + Very Aware Customer Review: Biweekly

Action:Technical support in closing meeting

Tool:Technical presentation + case analysis

Metric:Technical meeting NPS > 8
