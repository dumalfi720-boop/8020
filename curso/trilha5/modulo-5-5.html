🚀 Strategy for Very Conscious

The person has already decided to buy from you. Only the final trigger is missing. Provide clarity, real urgency, and a frictionless path to closure.

Topic 1

✅ What characterizes this level

The very conscious and the dream client:Have you already decided that you want to buy from you?. Knows the product, trusts you, understands the value. It just needs one final push — a condition, a moment, an ease.

Signs of a very conscious

  • ✓Question about payment methods and installments
  • ✓Ask for details about starting and onboarding
  • ✓Returns to the website or sales page several times
  • ✓Respond to emails and interact with offer content

📊 Fatal error

The biggest mistake with very conscious andcontinue educating instead of selling. He already knows everything he needs to know. More educational content at this point delays the decision. Be direct.

Topic 2
2

🎯 Clear and direct offer

For the very conscious,clarity and the greatest virtue. No long sales letters, no “but wait, there’s more!” Say exactly: what he receives, how much it costs, how he pays and when he starts.

Direct Offer Structure

1

What do you get

Clear and objective list of deliverables: "Diagnosis + Plan + 4 sessions + 90-day follow-up"

2

How much does it cost

Transparent price: "R$4,997 in cash or 12x R$497"

3

Guarantee

"30 days unconditional guarantee. If you don't like it, we'll give you a 100% refund."

4

Next step

"Click the button below. You will receive access within 24 hours."

Topic 3
3

⏳ Real urgency

Urgency for the very conscious works — as long as it isreal. Really limited spaces, bonuses that actually expire, prices that will really go up. False scarcity destroys trust instantly.

✓ Real and ethical urgency

  • "Class closes on the 15th — we serve a maximum of 10 companies per cycle"
  • "VIP onboarding bonus available this week only"
  • "Launch price until Friday. Then it will return to normal price"
  • "Real vacancies with limited service capacity"

✗ Fake urgency (don't do this)

  • "Last spots!" (repeats every week)
  • Fake timer that resets when recharging
  • "Never this price again" (comes back every month)
  • Invented scarcity with no real basis

💡 Rule of Thumb

If you can't justify the urgency with a real fact,don't use urgency. It's better to sell without pressure than to sell with a lie. Customers who purchase through manipulation are the first to ask for a refund.

Topic 4
4

📢 Call to action

The CTA for very conscious people should bedirect, clear and unambiguous. It's not "learn more" — it's "buy now." It's not "schedule a conversation" — it's "guarantee your spot". The next step should be obvious and unique.

CTAs that convert very consciously

E-mail
I want to start now
Sales page
Secure my place
WhatsApp
Close now
Checkout
Buy safely

📊 Principle

Each additional click between decision and purchase reduces conversion by20-30%. Simplify as much as possible. The ideal: 1 button → 1 checkout page → purchase made.

Topic 5
5

💬 Examples of communication for the very conscious

Communication for very conscious people is the shortest and most direct of all.Less text, more clarity, immediate action.

Example 1: Last Chance Email

Subject: Today is the last day

"John,

Registration for the milestone class closes today at 11:59 pm.

You receive: Complete diagnosis + 4 implementation sessions + 90 days of monitoring + Bonus: business process template.

Investment: R$4,997 or 12x R$497. 30-day guarantee.

→ [SECURE MY SPOT]

Next class only in June."

Example 2: Closing WhatsApp Message

"Hi João! Remember that you asked to let me know when the class opened? It opened today and we have 3 vacancies. Do you want me to send you the registration link?"

→ Simple, personal, no pressure but with real scarcity.

Example 3: Retargeting Ad

Announcement for those who visited the sales page:

"You were almost there. Last places for the March 8020 class. 30-day guarantee. [FINALIZE REGISTRATION]"

→ No long copy. No education. Just reminder + facilitation.

💡 Practical Exercise

Write 1 closing email and 1 WhatsApp message to your very conscious ones. Test: if someone who already wants to buy reads, is the next step 100% clear? If not, simplify.
