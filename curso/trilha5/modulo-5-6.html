⚖️ The 60/40 rule

The ideal proportion of content and60% education/value and 40% offer/conversion. This keeps the audience engaged without seeming like you just want to sell, but without missing opportunities to convert.

Visual Distribution

60% Education
40% Offer

60% Education includes:

Stories, data, tutorials, comparisons, behind the scenes, opinions

40% Offer includes:

Cases with CTA, testimonials, direct offers, launches, promos

📊 Why this proportion?

Who posts100% educationbuilds audience but never converts. Who posts100% offeralienates the audience. 60/40 is the balance proven by years of content marketing.

Topic 5

🤖 Automation vs human

Not everything needs to be manual, but not everything can be automated. The key is knowingWhat to automate and what to keep personalto scale without losing quality.

🤖 Automate

  • Nurturing email sequences
  • Scheduled posts on social media
  • Initial automatic responses
  • Lead segmentation by behavior
  • Standardized follow-ups

👤 Keep it Personal

  • Closing conversations
  • Responding to complex objections
  • Negotiation and customized proposals
  • Onboarding new customers
  • Relationship with VIP customers

📊 General rule

Automate what andrepetitive and predictable. Keep what is humanrelational and decisive. Top and middle of the funnel: automate. Bottom of the funnel: humanize.
