Topic 1

😴 Template for Unconscious

O unconscious does not know there is a problem. He is not looking for a solution because he does not know he needs one. Content for this audience must arouse curiosity without selling.

TEMPLATE: Content for the Unconscious

Criterion Specification
Type of content Entertainment, curiosities, surprising facts, everyday stories
Main channel Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, viral LinkedIn posts
Frequency 5-7x per week (high volume, short format)
Tone Light, curious, provocative. Without mentioning product or service.
CTA Follow, share, comment. No sales CTA.
Example "Did you know that 80% of companies lose sales because they don't have a commercial process? Here's something that will surprise you..." (30s video with impactful data)

💡 Success Metric

Reach and engagement (likes, shares, comments).Don't expect sales from this content. The objective is to attract an audience and plant seeds. The harvest comes in the following levels.

Topic 2
2

😟 Problem Aware Template

This publicYou know you have a problem but you don't know how to solve it. He seeks information, consumes educational content and is open to learning. It's time to name the pain and amplify it.

TEMPLATE: Content for Aware of the Problem

Criterion Specification
Type of content Educational posts, lists of common errors, diagnoses, "signs that you have this problem"
Main channel Blog, Instagram carousel, YouTube (5-10 min videos), newsletter
Frequency 3-5x per week (medium format, dense content)
Tone Empathetic, educational, diagnostic. Show that you understand the pain.
CTA Download material, sign up, schedule a free diagnosis.
Example "5 signs that your sales team is selling less than they should (and what to do about it)" — carousel on Instagram with 7 slides

🔎 Template for Solution Aware

This publicYou know there are solutions but you still don't know yours. You are comparing options, researching alternatives. The content should position you as the best choice.

TEMPLATE: Content for Solution Aware

Criterion Specification
Type of content Success stories, comparisons, demonstrations, webinars, customer interviews
Main channel YouTube (long videos), webinars, email marketing, LinkedIn articles
Frequency 2-3x per week (long format, high depth)
Tone Authority, proof, trust. Shows concrete results.
CTA Schedule a demonstration, speak to a consultant, watch the full case.

Topic 4
4

🎁 Product Aware Template

This publicYou already know your product but haven't bought it yet. It's almost there. The content must remove the last objections and create the final push.

TEMPLATE: Content for Product Aware

Criterion Specification
Type of content Video testimonials, detailed FAQ, guarantees, live demonstrations, behind the scenes
Main channel Email marketing, remarketing, WhatsApp, sales page, stories
Frequency 1-2x per week (varied format, high persuasion)
Tone Transparent, confident, direct. Shows what the customer will receive.
CTA Buy, start now, enjoy special conditions.
Example "See what Joao said after 3 months using the 8020 on his team" — 2 min video testimonial + direct link to purchase
Topic 5
5

🔥 Template for Very Conscious

O very aware already want to buy. It just needs the final push. You don't need more education — you need the ease to act now.

TEMPLATE: Content for Very Conscious

Criterion Specification
Type of content Direct offers, special conditions, bonuses, countdown, launch
Main channel Direct email, WhatsApp, call, remarketing with offer
Frequency Punctual and concentrated (maximum 3-7 day window)
Tone Direct, urgent, scarce. No fluff — get straight to the point.
CTA Buy now, secure your spot, enjoy before it's gone.
Example "Last 5 vacancies with implementation bonus included. Access closes Friday at 11:59 pm. Link in bio." — stories with countdown

Consolidated Vision: Content Funnel

Top
😴 Unconscious → Entertainment, data, curiosity (5-7x/week)
Mid-high
😟 Problem → Education, diagnosis, common errors (3-5x/week)
Quite
🔎 Solution → Cases, comparisons, demonstrations (2-3x/week)
Mid-low
🎁 Product → Testimonials, guarantees, FAQ (1-2x/week)
Bottom
🔥 Very conscious → Direct offer, urgency, CTA (punctual)

💡 Fatal Error

The most common mistake is making bottom-of-the-funnel content (offer) for the top (unconscious) audience. And how to propose on the first date.Each level needs the right content. Respect the journey.
