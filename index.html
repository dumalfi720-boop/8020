The 3 Axes of the Course

The 3 Axes of the Course

This course does not teach sales in a linear way. It works in 3 dimensions at the same time.

🎯
Axis 1 — The Salesperson
Who sells. Not everyone is the same — different profiles need different paths.
→Talented: refine and give autonomy
→Trainable: script, method, repetition
→Resistant: reposition function
→No fit: redirect
👤
Axis 2 — The Customer
For those who sell. 5 levels of consciousness that require different communication.
→Unconscious: educate and identify
→Aware of the problem: deepening pain
→Solution aware: differentiate
→Product Aware: Prove
→Very conscious: close
📊
Axis 3 — The Manager
Who coordinates. Diagnoses the team, understands the customer, crosses the two.
→Diagnose each seller
→Set right track by profile
→Combine seller + customer + approach
→Train, measure and adjust

The central idea:The course doesn't just focus on "how to sell". Focus onhow to build a trading systemwhere salesperson, customer and manager are aligned — and all levels of consciousness are worked on all the time.
